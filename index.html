STARGAZER

Hold your phone up to the night sky, or drag to look around. StarGazer computes exactly where the stars, planets, Moon and famous deep-sky objects are above you right now — tap any of them to learn its name, distance and story.

Your location stays on your device and is only used to compute the local sky. Positions are accurate to about a degree.
